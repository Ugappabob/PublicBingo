Manual Migration (Alternative)

If the automatic migration doesn't work, you can manually copy your lists:

Manual Migration Steps:

  1. Go to your development site (localhost:3000)
  2. Open browser console (F12)
  3. Run: JSON.stringify(JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('userContributions')), null, 2)
  4. Copy the output
  5. Go to production site (publicbingo.web.app)
  6. Open browser console (F12)
  7. Run: localStorage.setItem('userContributions', '[paste your data here]')
  8. Refresh the page
